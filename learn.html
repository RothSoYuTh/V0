Learn how to protect yourself from online scams

Common selling scam tactics in Cambodia

Deposit scam

Seller asks for deposit to hold payment before meeting. After receiving money, they disappear or block you.

Example:

"Send me 50% deposit to reserve the item"

"Transfer first so I know you're serious"

Too Good to Be True

Prices far below market value for popular tech like iPhones, laptops, or electronics.

Example:

"iPhone 15 Pro only $200"

"Brand new MacBook 2023 model only $300"

Urgency Pressure

Uses fake urgency to prevent you from thinking critically or doing research.

Example:

"Last one in stock, must decide now!"

"Special price today only. Ends in 1 hour!"

Fake Proof

Shows fake receipts, screenshots of other "satisfied customers", or doctored images.

Example:

"Look at all these 5-star reviews!"

"Here's proof of my last successful transaction."

Payment Method Red Flags

Insists on non-reversible payment methods that offer no buyer protection.

Example:

"Only accept bank transfer or gift cards"

"Pay via Western Union or cryptocurrency only"

Profile Red Flags

New account, no profile photo, limited activity, or suspicious profile patterns.

Example:

"Account created this week"

"No friends/followers or engagement"

Safe shopping practice

DO These Things

  • Check Seller's Profile Age And Activity History
  • Use This Platform To Check If Seller Was Reported
  • Meet In Public Places And Verify Product Before Payment
  • Inspect The Item Thoroughly Before Paying
  • Use Payment Methods With Buyer Protection
  • Get Their Real Name, ID, Phone For Real Contact
  • Research Market Prices To Verify Fair Deals
  • Trust Your Instincts - If It Feels Wrong, Walk Away

DON'T Do These Things

  • Don't Send Payment Before Receiving The Item
  • Don't Deposits To Sellers You Haven't Met
  • Don't Pay Cash Without Getting ID Verification
  • Don't Meet In Secluded Or Unfamiliar Locations
  • Don't Wire Transfers, Payment Requests Or Crypto
  • Don't Ignore That Seems Too Good To Be True
  • Don't Rush Decisions Due To Urgency Tactics
  • Don't Ignore Red Flags Regarding Everything Will Be Fine

Secure payment method

Cash on Delivery

safe

Pay only after inspecting the item in person.

ABA/Wing Escrow

safe

Use escrow services where payment is held until delivery is confirmed.

Credit/Debit Card

moderate

Some cards offer buyer protection. Use for reputable sites.

Bank Transfer

Risky

Hard to reverse. Only use with verified sellers.

Gift Cards

Very Risky

Never buy gift cards to pay for purchases. Common scam method.

Crypto Currency

Very Risky

Nearly impossible to recover. Avoid for online shopping.
